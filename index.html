Dear Daddy Jelian,

this is me, Mei Mei… your chaotic little girl who still misses you.

I never knew how to say goodbye without feeling like I’m losing something I never had.

Somewhere between distance and silence… I fell for you softly, dangerously quiet.

We didn’t end because we stopped caring… but because reality didn’t match what we felt.

Wise men say… only fools rush in.

but I couldn’t help falling in love with you.

This is me letting you go…

not because I stopped loving you,
but because I finally understand
some people are only meant to be felt, not kept.

goodbye… for real, and for the last.
