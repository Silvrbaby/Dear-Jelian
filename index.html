Dear Daddy Jelian, this is me… your sweet, chaotic little girl who still misses you in ways she doesn’t always know how to explain, Mei Mei. I kept trying to find a way to say this without making it sound like I’m giving up on you— but the truth is… this isn’t me giving up. this is me finally being honest with a feeling that grew quietly, gently, and a little too deeply. somewhere between our late conversations, the distance that never felt real until it did, and all the almost moments we never got to finish— I fell for you. not loudly, not all at once, but in soft pieces… that somehow became everything. and that’s why this hurts the way it does. because nothing actually broke between us. no anger, no ending we could point at— just reality… slowly pulling us apart. I think a part of me will always wonder what we could’ve been if distance wasn’t this cruel, or if time had been a little kinder to us. I gave you that music box in a rush, but maybe that’s because some feelings don’t wait to be perfect. and if you ever listen to it, really listen— maybe you’ll hear the part of me that didn’t know how to stay quiet anymore: Wise men say… only fools rush in, but I couldn’t help falling in love with you. maybe in another life, I’ll find you again— a softer version of me, a closer version of you, and a world that finally lets us happen. but in this life… this is where I let you go. not because I stopped loving you, but because loving you from here is slowly breaking me in ways you can’t see. so I’ll carry you in the quiet parts of my days, in songs that suddenly feel heavier, and in the kind of memories that don’t ask to be forgotten. and this… this is my goodbye. for real, and for the last. please take care of yourself, for me… even if I’m no longer there to remind you.
